# Free Grain Pricing Tool

A practical Excel model for grain growers to calculate break-even
price and margin per tonne during harvest.

## Download
[Download the Excel model](Harvest_Pricing_Locked_Model.xlsx)

## Why this exists
Grain markets move quickly during harvest. This tool helps growers
make calm, margin-based decisions using their own costs and yields.

## Features
- Locked formulas
- Cost per tonne calculation
- Margin per tonne
- Simple price scenarios
- Free and non-commercial
